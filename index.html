Welldrop

You're on the run! You find yourself having to jump down a well (a dry one with a sparkling tiled floor). What do you do? You reach for your phone, is what you do. You fire up the trusty Welldrop and measure the depth of that hole alright.

OK I admit, that's an unlikely scenario. Welldrop lets you measure heights or depths. All you need is a small coin! Here's a demo:

Show Guide

  • Pick the smallest coin you can find.
  • Open Welldrop and start a new measurement.
  • Hold the phone horizontally at the height you want to measure.
  • Hold the coin between your index finger and thumb, approx. 0.5 cm (1/4") above the edge of your phone, preferably close to the microphone.
  • Hold the coin at a 30-45 degree angle towards the phone.
  • Holding it horizontally or any other way will result in inaccurate measurement.
  • Drop the coin ensuring it hits the phone on its edge.
  • Make sure the coin flips as it falls.
  • When the coin hits the floor, your phone should vibrate and show the height.
  • Make sure the sound of coin hitting the floor is loud. If you have carpet, put a sheet of paper on it and let the coin fall on it.
